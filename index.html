BTS, or Bangtan Sonyeondan (Beyond The Scene), is a Grammy-nominated South Korean boy band that debuted in June 2013. They have gained global fame for their self-produced music and record-breaking achievements. Known as "21st Century Pop Icons", they have earned four No. 1 songs, sold-out stadiums, and been named TIME's Entertainer of the Year 2020. Their dedicated fan base, ARMY (Adorable Representative M.C. for Youth), is known for spreading love and positivity worldwide.
