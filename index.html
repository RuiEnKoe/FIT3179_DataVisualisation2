BTS, or Bangtan Sonyeondan (Beyond The Scene), is a Grammy-nominated South Korean boy band that debuted in June 2013. They have gained global fame for their self-produced music and record-breaking achievements. Known as "21st Century Pop Icons", they have earned four No. 1 songs, sold-out stadiums, and been named TIME's Entertainer of the Year 2020. Their dedicated fan base, ARMY (Adorable Representative M.C. for Youth), is known for spreading love and positivity worldwide.

The TrendSetters

BTS has emerged as the ultimate trendsetters in KPOP and even globally. They have achieved a level of global influence and success that no other bands have come close to matching. As the first KPOP group to break into the U.S. music market, they laid the foundation for KPOP's global reach.

The group's impact on KPOP is unparalleled, as they have redefined the genre's boundaries and paved the way for future generation of artists, while expanding the global reach of KPOP. Their unmatched success continues to inspire other KPOP bands, shaping the future of the industry.
